Rules

Dune: Imperium is a game of strategy and political intrigue. Players take on the roles of noble houses vying for control of the planet Arrakis, the only known source of the spice melange. Players must use their influence, cunning, and military might to outmaneuver their opponents and secure their place as the dominant power in the Dune universe.

The game is played over a series of rounds, each of which is divided into three phases:

  1. Agent Phase: Players send their agents to different locations on the game board to gather support, wage war, or manipulate the Landsraad.
  2. Action Phase: Players take turns playing cards from their hands to perform actions. Actions can be used to gather resources, build units, attack opponents, or gain political power.
  3. End Phase: Players collect income, produce melange, and check for victory.

The game ends when one player has achieved victory by controlling a majority of the territories on Arrakis, or by having the most victory points at the end of the game.
